1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Haze ("the App," "we," "our," or "us"),
developed by Noah McClung, handles information when you use the Haze iOS
weather application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Haze is
designed to collect only what is strictly necessary to provide weather
information, and no personal data is collected, stored, or sold by us.

By using Haze, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.


2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Location Data

Haze requests access to your device's location through Apple's CoreLocation
framework. Location data is used solely to fetch current weather conditions
and forecasts for your present position. Specifically:

  - When you grant "While Using the App" location permission, your device's
    GPS coordinates are passed directly to Apple's WeatherKit service to
    retrieve weather data.
  - Your location coordinates are never stored by us, never transmitted to our
    servers (we operate no servers), and never shared with any third party
    other than Apple's WeatherKit service as described in Section 4 below.
  - You may also manually search for and select a city instead of sharing your
    device location. In that case, no GPS coordinates are accessed.

2.2 City Search Queries

When you type a city name in the location search feature, your search text is
passed to Apple's MapKit framework (MKLocalSearchCompleter) to generate
autocomplete suggestions. We do not log, store, or transmit your search
queries to any server we operate.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Haze does not collect, process, or store:
  - Your name, email address, or any account information
  - Browsing history or usage analytics
  - Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  - Device identifiers or fingerprinting data
  - Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to our servers
  - Payment or financial information
  - Any data from your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or
tracking libraries.


3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

The only data Haze processes is your device location (when permitted), and it
is used exclusively to:

  - Fetch real-time weather conditions for your current position via Apple
    WeatherKit
  - Display a 5-day weather forecast for your current position

Location data is used in-memory only for the duration of the weather request
and is discarded immediately after the response is received.


4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Haze relies on the following Apple-provided services, each governed by Apple's
own Privacy Policy (https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/):

  - Apple WeatherKit: Receives your device coordinates to return weather data.
    WeatherKit is subject to Apple's WeatherKit terms and privacy practices.
  - Apple MapKit / MKLocalSearchCompleter: Processes city search text to
    return location suggestions. This service is governed by Apple's privacy
    practices.

We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content,
privacy policies, or practices of Apple's services. We encourage you to review
Apple's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party services, SDKs, or APIs are integrated into Haze.


5. DATA RETENTION

We do not retain any personal data. Because Haze operates without a backend
server or database, no information about you or your usage is stored anywhere
by us. All location lookups are stateless and ephemeral.


6. DATA SHARING AND SALE

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your information to any
third party. The only data transmission that occurs is the passing of your
device location to Apple WeatherKit, as described above, which is necessary
for the core function of the App.


7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Haze is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly

8. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS AND CHOICES

8.1 Location Permission

You can grant or revoke location access at any time through your device
Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Haze. Revoking location
access will not affect your ability to use the App; you may still manually
search for any city.

8.2 California Residents (CCPA)

Because Haze does not collect personal information as defined under the
California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), there is no personal information to
access, delete, or opt out of selling. We do not sell personal information.

8.3 European Residents (GDPR)

Because Haze does not collect or store personal data, the General Data
Protection Regulation (GDPR) obligations related to data access, deletion, and
portability do not apply to data held by us. To the extent Apple processes your
location data via WeatherKit on our behalf, Apple acts as a data processor
subject to GDPR. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.


9. DATA SECURITY

Although we do not store any of your data, we take reasonable measures to
ensure the App transmits data securely using Apple's native networking
frameworks, which enforce HTTPS/TLS for all communications.


10. ATTRIBUTION

Weather data in Haze is provided by Apple Weather. Please note that WeatherKit
requires acknowledgment of Apple as the data source in apps distributed on the
App Store.
